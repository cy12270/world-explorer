Travel Tips

Practical tips to make your trip safer and more enjoyable.

Packing Smart
  • Pack versatile clothing and layer for changing weather.
  • Carry photocopies of important documents and a digital backup.
  • Bring a compact first-aid kit and essential medications.
Safety & Security
  • Register travel plans with someone you trust and share your location.
  • Keep valuables secure and be aware of common local scams.
  • Use hotel safes and lockable luggage when possible.
Money & Documents
  • Carry a mix of payment methods (cash, card, mobile) and notify your bank.
  • Know local currency and typical tipping customs.
  • Keep passport scans in your email or cloud storage.
Health & Wellbeing
  • Check required vaccines and travel advisories for your destination.
  • Stay hydrated, protect from sun, and rest when crossing time zones.
  • Purchase travel insurance that covers medical evacuation.
Local Culture
  • Learn a few basic phrases and respect local customs and dress codes.
  • Ask permission before photographing people or sensitive sites.
  • Support local businesses and follow conservation rules.
Transportation
  • Confirm pickup times, fares, and official IDs for taxis and rideshares.
  • Use local transit apps or official websites to check schedules.
  • Allow extra time for connections and unexpected delays.
