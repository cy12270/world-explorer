Tbilisi, Georgia

Sitting at the historic crossroads of Europe and Asia, Georgia's capital, Tbilisi, is a city of intoxicating contrasts. Its dramatic valley setting is home to a labyrinthine Old Town where Eastern Orthodox churches, ornate mosques, and grand synagogues share the same cobblestone streets. This eclectic architecture reflects a long and complex history of Persian and Russian rule. Discover the city's legendary sulfur baths, explore its burgeoning scene of underground clubs and speakeasy-style bars, and indulge in a unique culinary tradition.
