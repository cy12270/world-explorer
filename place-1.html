Medellín, Colombia

Once infamous for its dangerous past, Medellín has undergone one of the most incredible urban transformations in modern history. Nestled in a lush green valley and dubbed the "City of Eternal Spring" for its perfect year-round climate, it is now a beacon of innovation, culture, and resilience. Visitors can ride the famous Metrocable system up the mountainside for stunning views, providing transport and connection for local communities. Explore the vibrant Comuna 13, once the city's most dangerous neighbourhood, now a canvas for colourful street art and a symbol of peace and hope. With its buzzing nightlife, world-class coffee, and the warm, welcoming spirit of its people, Medellín offers a profound and inspiring travel experience.
