Ljubljana, Slovenia

Often overlooked for its more famous European neighbours, Ljubljana is a true hidden gem. This city, whose name translates to "the beloved," feels like it was plucked from a storybook with its hilltop medieval castle overlooking a vibrant Old Town, charming bridges adorned with dragons, and the emerald-green Ljubljanica River winding through its heart. As the European Green Capital of 2016, the city centre is largely car-free, making it a joy to explore its lively riverside cafes, bustling markets, and thriving arts scene on foot or by bike. It’s a perfect base for exploring Slovenia’s breathtaking natural beauty, from the ethereal Lake Bled to the vast Postojna Caves.
